Public Transportation Routes from various Location

From Cubao

Board the LRT 2 at Cubao Araneta Station bound for Recto Station. The Train fare from Cubao Station to Recto Station is 20 pesos. Alight at Recto Station then take the walkway bridge to get to Doroteo Jose Station LRT 1. At the station, board the train bound for Baclaran. Alight at Central Terminal 2 stops later. The train fare from Doroteo Jose Station to Central Terminal Station is 15 pesos. From the station walk 400 meters towards Manila City Hall. Take the underpass in front of the building and follow the path to C. Victoria Street to get to the entrance of Intramuros.

From Monumento Caloocan

Route 1: The fastest way to go to Intramuros from Caloocan is by riding LRT 1 at Monumento Station. Alight at Central Station 9 stops later. The fare is 20 pesos. From the station, walk towards Liwasang Bonifacio or Manila City Hall. Go under the Manila City Hall underpass to cross Taft Avenue and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Take the path of C. Victoria Street. It will lead you to the East side entrance of Intramuros. The entrance will lead you to The Bayleaf Hotel, Manila High School, and Mapua Institute of Technology (MIT).

Route 2: From Monumento, ride a Jeepney with a signboard Baclaran or Gil Puyat. Tell the driver that you will alight at Intramuros. Usually, the driver will drop you off at the center island of the Manila City Hall underpass. The Jeepney Fare is 20 pesos. From there take the underpass to cross Taft Avenue and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Afterward, follow the path of C. Victoria Street as it will lead you to the East side entrance of Intramuros.

From SM City North EDSA Quezon City

Route 1: From the mall, ride a Jeepney or bus towards Balintawak then alight at Roosevelt LRT 1 Station. The jeepney fare is 12 pesos. Ride the train from Roosevelt Station bound for Baclaran Station. Then get off at Central Station 11 stops later. The train fare is 30 pesos. Walk towards Manila City Hall, take the underpass to cross Taft Avenue, and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Follow the C. Victoria Street to get to the East Entrance of Intramuros.

Route 2: At West Avenue ride a Jeepney with a signboard Manila City Hall / T.M. Kalaw / Luneta / Lawton. Tell the driver to drop you off in front of the City Hall building. The Jeepney fare is 30 pesos. From there use the underpass in the center island to get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Follow the path to Victoria Street as it will lead you to the entrance of Intramuros.

From SM Fairview Quezon City

Ride any jeepney or UV Express bound to T.M. Kalaw then alight at Manila City Hall. The driver will usually drop you off at the center island before the Lagusnilad bridge. Use the underpass to cross Taft Avenue and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. From there take the path of Victoria Street to enter Intramuros.

From Malanday Valenzuela

Route 1: Ride Jeepney bound to Manila with signboard Pier 15/ Lawton / Post Office. The estimated one-way Jeepney fare is 34 pesos. Tell the driver that you will alight at Intramuros. Usually, the driver will drop you off at the Northern entrance of Intramuros which is along the streets of Magallanes Drive and Soriano Avenue. This entrance will lead you to the Bureau of Immigration, Fort Santiago, Manila Cathedral, San Agustin Church, Comelec Annex Building, and Palacio del Gobernador.

Route 2: From Malanday ride a jeepney bound for Monumento LRT station. The fare is 20 pesos. Ride the train at the Monumento Station bound for Baclaran. After 9 stops, alight at Central Terminal Station. The Train Fare is 20 pesos. From the station, walk towards Manila City Hall, take the underpass to cross Taft Avenue, and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Follow the path of C. Victoria Street to enter Intramuros from the Eastern entrance.

From Pasay City

Route 1: Board the train bound for Monumento at LRT 1 EDSA Station then alight at Central Station 7 stops later. The Train Fare is 20 pesos. From the latter, walk towards Manila City Hall or Liwasang Bonifacio. In front of the City Hall, take the underpass to cross the TAFT Avenue Road to get to Padre Burgos Avenue. From there walk towards C. Victoria Street. Follow the street to enter Intramuros via the Eastern Entrance.

Route 2: Ride Jeep with signboards bound to Monumento / Divisoria / Sta. Cruz / Blumentritt /Recto / Quiapo via Taft Avenue. Tell the driver that you will alight at Manila City Hall. The Jeepney fare is 19 pesos. From there take the city hall underpass to cross Taft Avenue and get to Padre Burgos Street. Afterward, follow the path of C. Victoria Street to enter Intramuros.

From Makati

Route 1: Ride a Jeep bound to Buendia Avenue corner Taft Avenue. The fare is 12 pesos. From there board the LRT 1 at Gil Puyat station bound for Monumento then alight at Central Station 5 stations later. The train fare from Gil Puyat to Central Terminal is 20 pesos. Afterward, walk towards Manila City Hall, take the underpass next to it to cross Taft Avenue, and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Follow the part of C. Victoria Street to get to Intramuros.

Route 2: Ride Jeep bound to Buendia Avenue corner Taft Avenue. From there ride another jeep with signboards Sta. Cruz / Blumentritt / Monumento. Tell the driver that you will alight at Manila City Hall. From there follow the walking instruction from Route 1.

From Laguna

Ride any Laguna provincial bus bound for Buendia / Gil Puyat in Pasay City in Metro Manila. Alight at the foot of Gil Puyat LRT station on Taft Avenue. The Bus Fare is 130 pesos (estimated). From there you can board LRT 1 at Gil Puyat station then alight at Central Station. The Train Fare is 20 pesos. Or from Gil Puyat, you can ride a Jeepney bound for Manila City Hall. The Jeepney Fare is 18 pesos. From the City Hall take the underpass to cross Taft Avenue and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Afterward, follow the path of C. Victoria Street to get to the entrance of Intramuros.

From Alabang

Ride Bus bound for Pasay Rotonda in EDSA. Alight at Pasay Rotonda then walk towards Taft Avenue. The bus fare is 50 pesos (estimated). Board the LRT 1 Train at EDSA station bound for Monumento. Alight at Central Station 7 stops later. The Train Fare is 20 pesos. From there walk towards Manila City Hall. Take the underpass to get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Afterward, follow the part of C. Victoria Street to enter Intramuros.

From Bulacan

Route 1: Ride any provincial busses bound for Doroteo Jose in Santa Cruz Manila. Ride jeep with signboards A. Mabini / Manila City Hall / SM Manila / Taft Avenue / Pasay / Baclaran. Tell the driver to drop you off at Lawton or in Manila City Hall. From there walk towards the Entrance of Intramuros which is just a stone's throw away from Lawton or City Hall.

Route 2: Ride provincial buses going to D. Jose LRT station. Board the LRT 1 train at D. Jose Station bound for Baclaran. Alight at Central Terminal 2 stops later. The Train Fare is 15 pesos. From the station walk towards Liwasang Bonifacio or Manila City Hall. Take the city hall underpass to cross Taft Avenue and get to Padre Burgos Avenue. Follow the path towards C. Victoria Street as it leads you to the entrance of Intramuros.
